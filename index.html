WELCOME TO THAKUR INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Welcome to Thakur International School, where education is a journey of discovery, integrity, and achievement. We are committed to nurturing confident learners, responsible citizens, and compassionate leaders prepared to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

Rooted in strong values and driven by academic excellence, our school provides an enriching environment where every child is encouraged to explore, question, create, and grow.

Vision & Mission

Vision

  • To promote all young learners towards becoming responsible global citizens who are intellectual and ethical in their approach to their own lives and the world.

Mission

  • To provide holistic education that nurtures scientific temper and ethical values, grooming the students to be worthy and responsible citizens.

Our Campus

A world class learning environment designed to inspire imagination, creativity and exploration. Every space supports learning and innovation.

Modern Classrooms and Smart Learning Tools

Air-conditioned classrooms equipped with advanced digital tools for interactive and engaging learning experiences.

Well Equipped Science and Computer Labs

Advanced laboratories for Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Robotics that encourage hands on inquiry, experimentation, and technological innovation.

Reading Zones (Library, E-Library and Toy Library)

A well spacious and resource-rich library, advanced E-library and engaging toy library, which provide a stimulating environment for integrated learning.

AR-VR Lab

Our State-of-the-art AR-VR Lab transforms traditional lessons into immersive, hands-on experiences where students do not only learn concepts — they experience them.

Health and Wellness Facilities

A well-equipped First Aid Room with essential facilities staffed by trained personnel. Counselling services support students' emotional, mental and academic growth.

Safety and Security

Round the clock CCTV surveillance, trained security personnel, and secure entry and exit systems ensure a safe and nurturing environment at all times.

Bus Facility and GPS Tracking

GPS and CCTV enabled school transport facility. This facility makes travelling safe and comfortable for students. It relieves anxiety in today's age of career and business oriented parents.

Co-Curricular Activities and Student Life

At Thakur International School, education extends beyond academics. We believe, true growth happens when students explore passions, discover talents, and build meaningful relationships.

Sports and Physical Education

A vibrant playground offering cricket, football, basketball, Athletics, swimming, badminton, table tennis, carrom and chess that foster teamwork, sportsmanship, discipline, and physical well-being.

Creative Art Room and Performance Spaces

Dedicated art, music, dance, speech and drama rooms where students confidently explore and showcase their creative talents. This brings conviction, ingenuity and stage presence.

Leadership Programs

Through Student Council, Debates, Competitions, and Special Assemblies, we nurture leadership, responsibility and empathy. These opportunities strengthen self-belief and create well-rounded individuals.

High Success Rate

Our students are consistently ranked among the top performers in the city, confidently progressing to prestigious higher education institutions and successful future paths.

Admission Process

store

Admission Process - Offline

1

Obtaining the Brochure and application form for Admission, from the School office.

2

Submitting the duly completed application form and supporting documents to TIS Administrative office and paying the Registration fee within the specified period.

3

Informing about the admission decision by the school to the parents.

4

Acceptance of the admission by the parents and payment of applicable fees/charges/expenses.

5

Confirmation of admission by Thakur International School.
